Privacy Policy

SellerIntel — by Sgech Digital

Last Updated: April 2026

Short version: SellerIntel processes your Flipkart, Meesho, and Amazon report files entirely on your device. We do not upload, store, or share your financial data with anyone.

1. Who We Are

SellerIntel is developed and maintained by Sgech Digital. For questions, contact us at sgech.digital@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Name, Email, and Mobile Number (for account verification and Pro plan sync).
  • Device Information: Device ID and Advertising ID (to prevent unauthorized sharing and to serve ads).
  • Usage Data: App features used and crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics.
  • Cost Data: Product costs you manually enter — stored locally on your device only.

3. Payments & Subscriptions

We offer paid Pro plans. All payments are processed securely through Google Play Billing or authorized payment gateways. We do not store your credit card or bank details on our servers.

4. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your Flipkart, Meesho, or Amazon report files
  • Your bank settlement amounts or transaction data
  • Your SKU names, pricing, or business business logic
  • Your marketplace account credentials

All report file processing happens 100% on your device. No seller data leaves your phone.

5. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage your account and Pro subscriptions.
  • To show relevant advertisements in the free version of the app.
  • To provide customer support via email or mobile.
  • To improve app performance using anonymous crash data.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following secure third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — For authentication and account data storage.
  • Google AdMob — To serve advertisements in the app.
  • Google Play Services — For updates and secure payment processing.

7. Your Rights

  • Access & Correction: Request your data or update it in settings.
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all data via Settings > Delete Account.
  • Portability: Export your data upon request.
