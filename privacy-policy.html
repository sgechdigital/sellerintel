Privacy Policy

SellerIntel — by Sgech Digital

Last Updated: March 2026

Short version: SellerIntel processes your Flipkart Excel files entirely on your device. We do not upload, store, or share your financial data with anyone.

1. Who We Are

SellerIntel is developed and maintained by Sgech Digital. For questions, contact us at sgech.digital@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use SellerIntel:

  • Account Information: Name and email address when you create an account.
  • Device Information: Device ID (to prevent unauthorized account sharing).
  • Usage Data: App features used, crash reports (via Firebase Crashlytics).
  • Cost Data: Product costs you manually enter — stored locally on your device.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your Flipkart Excel files or financial reports
  • Your bank settlement amounts or transaction data
  • Your SKU names, pricing, or business data
  • Your Flipkart account credentials

All Excel processing happens 100% on your device. No data leaves your phone.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the SellerIntel service
  • To verify your account and prevent unauthorized access
  • To send important app updates and security notices
  • To improve app performance using anonymous crash data
  • To show relevant advertisements (via Google AdMob)

5. Advertising

SellerIntel uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use your device's advertising ID to show personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings under Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

We do not share your personal financial data with advertisers.

6. Data Storage & Security

  • Account data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers
  • Your cost inputs are stored locally on your device
  • We use industry-standard encryption for all data transmission
  • We limit data access to authorized personnel only

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication and data storage (Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdMob — Advertising (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play Services — App delivery and updates

8. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update your name or email in app settings
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data from Settings → Delete Account
  • Portability: Export your data upon request

9. Children's Privacy

SellerIntel is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
