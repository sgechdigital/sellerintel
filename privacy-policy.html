Privacy Policy

SellerIntel — by Sgech Digital

Last Updated: March 2026

Short version: SellerIntel processes your Flipkart, Meesho, and Amazon report files entirely on your device. We do not upload, store, or share your financial data with anyone.

1. Who We Are

SellerIntel is developed and maintained by Sgech Digital. For questions, contact us at sgech.digital@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use SellerIntel:

  • Account Information: Name and email address when you create an account.
  • Device Information: Device ID (to prevent unauthorized account sharing).
  • Usage Data: App features used, crash reports (via Firebase Crashlytics).
  • Cost Data: Product costs you manually enter — stored locally on your device only.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your Flipkart, Meesho, or Amazon report files
  • Your bank settlement amounts or transaction data
  • Your SKU names, pricing, or business data
  • Your Flipkart, Meesho, or Amazon account credentials
  • Any financial or sales information from your uploaded files

All file processing happens 100% on your device. No seller data leaves your phone.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the SellerIntel service
  • To verify your account and prevent unauthorized access
  • To send important app updates and security notices
  • To improve app performance using anonymous crash data

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Account data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers
  • Your cost inputs are stored locally on your device only
  • Your uploaded report files are never transmitted to any server
  • We use industry-standard encryption for all data transmission
  • We limit data access to authorized personnel only

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication and account data storage (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play Services — App delivery and updates (Privacy Policy)

7. Meesho & Amazon Data

SellerIntel processes Meesho ZIP/CSV files and Amazon Transaction CSV files in addition to Flipkart Excel reports. All file processing happens locally on your device. No seller report data — including order details, settlement amounts, SKU information, or financial figures — is transmitted to our servers or any third party.

8. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data by emailing us
  • Correction: Update your name or email in app settings
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data from Settings → Delete Account
  • Portability: Export your data upon request via email

9. Children's Privacy

SellerIntel is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed within 30 days. Locally stored cost data on your device is removed when you uninstall the app.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

For privacy questions, data requests, or concerns:

  • Email: sgech.digital@gmail.com
  • Company: Sgech Digital
  • Response time: Within 7 business days
